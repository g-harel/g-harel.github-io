Sayra Who?

I'm Sayra Ashqar, born in the effortlessly cool city of Montreal, Canada into a family of die hard garment industry entrepreneurs. After attending graduate school in Miami, Florida, I moved to Manhattan to begin a career managing a well known, mental health outpatient treatment center for people with Co-Occurring disorders. The people that knew me, never understood how I fell into that line of work. I had always been the girl with the outspoken personality and bold sense of style, far better suited towards the family business. Looking back at those years, I knew I wanted a challenge and despite the grueling hours, constant political setback and little pay, the reasons I stayed in that industry was solely a result of the overwhelming sense of happiness I felt when watching clients achieve their personal milestones that kept me going.

Fast forward, a few years later and an eventual move from the city, to the suburbs of Westchester, my husband and I decided to start our family. After I had my first child, I decided to become a full time mom. I had no idea how much my lifestyle and identity was going to change. One day it was high heels, tailored suits and happy hours in the city and then all of a sudden my world was turned upside down, with a house filled of noisy toys and pacifiers in any possible hidden corner you can imagine. As a super mom, my lifestyle is complete organized chaos, whether it's cleaning up 10 spills of milk in under 5 minutes, a 5 minute pitstop to the grocery store that turns into 2 hours, or out on a sexy date night with my husband, I still want to look and feel fabulous.

I created Unapologetic Stylist, after realizing that so many of us lose ourselves at some point and perhaps stop prioritizing our style after having made major transitions.We are all trying to be our best, constantly balancing multiple duties between our personal and professional lives. Face it, life is complicated and messy but you on the other hand, will always be fabulous and so should your wardrobe!!!!
